About Us

Hi! Our names are Faith and Mitsuka, and we started this website to help students who may be struggling with stress. We are all part of Girls Who Code, and this site was created for a Community Impact Project that we are working on. Both of us are so excited to share our tips as students, and we hope this site will help you as much as it can.
